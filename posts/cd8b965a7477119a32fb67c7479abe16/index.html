@MathewSachin
Hi, let me first begin to say i really like your Captura project, and I’ve learned allot from it coding wise.
But i also encountered the a/v desync.
So i basically started from scratch to debug where the desync came from.
If you want i can share the project with you.

I think the desync comes from Audio part of the software.
You write all audio captures/loop-backs to an buffer which converts it to a certain wave format (if i’m correct).
I think the solution is to just pipe everything into ffmpeg and apply a filter ( “-filter_complex “[0:0][1:0]amerge=inputs=2[a];[2:0]scale=1280x720[v]” -map [a] -map [v]” 0 and 1 are audio streams 2 is video ) to merge all audio streams. And then maybe do the conversion as last step.

This might not be ideal as it seems you want to support multiple non-ffmpeg encoders/programs.
But then again: why do you need anything else then ffmpeg.

Cheers!
